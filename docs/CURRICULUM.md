# Curriculum Map

## Age band

Primary target: ages 5-9.

## Skill progression

### Hands
- Count objects to 20.
- Addition within 10.
- Addition within 20.
- Missing addend to 10.

### Feet
- Subtraction within 10.
- Subtraction within 20.
- Number-line jumps.
- Skip count by 2, 5, and 10.

### Eyes
- Compare numbers.
- Tens and ones.
- Place value to 100.
- Pattern recognition.

### Voice
- Tell time to hour.
- Tell time to half-hour.
- Tell time to quarter-hour.
- Simple sequencing.

### Heart
- Recognize coins.
- Make exact amounts.
- Compare prices.
- Simple multi-step word problems.

## Hint design

Hints should scaffold, not solve.

Example: 8 + 6

1. "Try making 10 first. 8 needs 2."
2. "Break 6 into 2 and 4."
3. "8 + 2 = 10, then 10 + 4 = 14."
